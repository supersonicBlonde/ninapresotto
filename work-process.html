How we can work together

A clear, structured, and transparent way to build your web project

Every successful digital project starts with a solid process.
My role is to turn your ideas into a high-performing website or application through a clear, transparent, and collaborative workflow. Here’s how it works, step by step.

01

Initial meeting — Understanding your goals

Goal:
fully grasp your project and priorities.

We start with a conversation (call or meeting) to discuss your business objectives — increasing sales, automating workflows, or improving user experience. I ask targeted questions, sign an NDA if needed, and clarify your expectations before moving forward.

Why it matters:
a deep understanding of your needs ensures that every technical decision serves your business goals.

02

Action plan and transparent quote

Goal:
define the scope and technical roadmap.

You’ll receive a detailed plan describing each project stage, the chosen technologies (Symfony, API, etc.), estimated deadlines, and deliverables.
The quote is clear and jargon-free — you know exactly what’s included.

Why it matters:
you get full visibility on what will be done, how, and at what cost.

03

Preparation and organization

Goal:
define the scope and technical roadmap.

I define milestones, create a shared project board (Trello, Slack, or similar), and gather all necessary assets — content, mockups, and access credentials.

Why it matters:
solid organization prevents delays and keeps you informed at every stage.

04

Technical development

Goal:
bring your project to life with clean, efficient code.

I build the front-end (HTML, CSS, JS) for a fast, responsive interface, and the back-end (Symfony, database, API) for a secure, scalable structure.
Each feature is tested continuously to ensure reliability.

Why it matters:
you get a robust and maintainable website that performs flawlessly.

05

Validation and adjustments

Goal:
ensure the result matches your expectations perfectly.

You test a beta version, share your feedback, and together we make any necessary refinements before the official launch.

Why it matters:
you stay in control, and the final version fully reflects your vision.

06

Launch

Goal:
deliver a project ready to perform from day one.

I deploy the site to your hosting environment, configure analytics, SEO, and security tools, and run final quality checks.

Why it matters:
everything is ready for immediate use — no downtime, no stress.

07

Maintenance and evolution

Goal:
keep your project secure and future-proof.

I remain available for support, bug fixes, and upgrades.
Maintenance plans can include performance monitoring, updates, and new features as your business evolves.

Why it matters:
your website stays fast, secure, and aligned with your growth.

Why this process stands out

  • Total transparency: every step is documented and explained.
  • Active collaboration: you stay involved without dealing with technical overload.
  • Tangible results: performance, reliability, and scalability drive every decision.
